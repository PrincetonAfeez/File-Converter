What we store

Account data (username, email) and the files you upload for conversion, plus the converted outputs and job metadata.

Retention

Converted outputs are deleted after the configured retention window; input files are purged after a job reaches a terminal state. Orphaned files are garbage-collected.

Your rights

You may request a copy of your data or deletion of your account and its data. Contact support or use the documented data-export / account-deletion procedures.

Subprocessors

If a webhook endpoint is configured, conversion event metadata is sent to it. No file contents are sent to third parties as part of the conversion pipeline.
